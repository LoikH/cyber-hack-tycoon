 # 👁️ CYBER-HACK TYCOON v1.0.4 👁️
An immersive, cinematic retro-cyberpunk OS simulation clicker.
Fully bilingual: Switch between English [EN] and French [FR] instantly with a single click!

---

### 💻 ENTER THE MATRIX
Take control of CyberOS, an advanced hacking terminal.
Type directly on your real physical keyboard to compile lines of code (LoC), sell malicious payloads
on the Black Market, and siphon funds to bypass global cybersecurity.

### 🛡️ CORE GAMEPLAY FEATURES:
- ⌨️ Immersive Keyboard Compiling: Press ANY key on your physical keyboard to inject realistic hacker commands!
- 👻 Evolutionary G.H.O.S.T. AI: Feed your AI companion with code to unlock powerful abilities (Overclock x2, Cash
Siphon, Threat Blackout).
- 🚨 NetWatch Emergency Lockdown: Keep your threat level below 100%, or trigger a full-screen critical panic mode
where you must manually purge your logs under pressure!
- ⚡ Active Breach Decryption (QTE): Catch network vulnerabilities in real-time and crack hexadecimal keys to
secure massive credit (฿) payloads.
- 🎵 Synthesized Web Audio: Satisfying mechanical keyboard sounds, alert sweeps, and notification pings generated
dynamically on-the-fly using the Web Audio API.
- 🏆 6 Hacking Achievements to unlock as you rise through the hacker ranks.
- 📱 Fully Responsive Layout: Play seamlessly in fullscreen mode, on desktop, or on mobile with a custom virtual
taskbar.

---

### 🛠️ HOW TO PLAY:
1. **Type or Click** to generate Lines of Code (LoC).
2. **Convert your LoC** into Credits (฿) in the Black Market.
3. **Upgrade your rig** with DDoS Botnets and Proxy VPNs to increase passive production and control network
threat.
4. **Interact with Commlink emails** to make branching narrative decisions and conquer target servers.
